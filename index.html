THE LEGACY OF 81

Four decades of breaking boundaries in the tattoo world.

Our Standards

Every artist at Studio 81 undergoes rigorous bloodborne pathogen training annually. We use single-use needles and specialized protective barriers for all equipment to ensure the highest safety standards in the industry.

Founded in Rebellion

In 1981, tattooing was a misunderstood art form. Our founder, Arthur "Eight-One" Vance, opened the first studio with a simple vision: treat tattoos like fine art in a gallery. This revolutionary approach changed how the city viewed body art forever.

Today, we carry that torch by blending traditional techniques with modern technology. From 3D-mapped designs to thermal stencil printing, we use every tool available to ensure precision.

Vision

To lead the global tattoo culture toward a future of artistic innovation and absolute safety.

Passion

We don't just "do tattoos." We collaborate with humans to externalize their internal identities.
